Example7.2.9

Find the left and right cosets of \(H=\langle (12)\rangle\) in \(S_3\text{.}\)

The left cosets are \begin{equation*}eH=H=(12)H,\end{equation*}\begin{equation*}(13)H=\{(13),(123)\}=(123)H,\end{equation*}\begin{equation*}\text{ and } (23)H=\{(23),(132)\}=(132)H,\end{equation*} and the right cosets are \begin{equation*}He=H=H(12),\end{equation*}\begin{equation*} H(13)=\{(13),(132)\}=H(132),\end{equation*}\begin{equation*}\text{ and } H(23)=\{(23),(123)\}=H(123).\end{equation*}

Thus, \(\siml\) partitions \(S_3\) into \(\{H,\{(13),(123)\},\{(23), (132)\}\}\) and \(\simr\) partitions \(S_3\) into \(\{H,\{(13),(132)\},\{(23), (123)\}\}\text{.}\)
