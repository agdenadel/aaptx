Definition7.1.3

Let \(S\) be a set. Then a relation on \(S\) is a subset \(R\) of \(S\times S\text{.}\) 2 If \(R\) is a relation on \(S\) and \(x,y\in S\text{,}\) then we say \(x\) is related to \(y\text{,}\) and write \(x R y\text{,}\) if \((x,y)\in R\text{;}\) otherwise, we say that \(x\) is not related to \(y\text{,}\) and write \(x \not R y\text{.}\)
