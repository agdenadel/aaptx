Next, let \(f,g,h\in F\text{.}\). Then for all \(x\in \R\text{,}\), \begin{align*} ((f+g)+h)(x)\amp =(f+g)(x)+h(x)\amp \amp\\ \amp =((f(x)+g(x))+h(x)\amp \amp\\ \amp =f(x)+(g(x)+h(x))\amp \amp \text{ (since addition is associative on \(\R\)) }\\ \amp =f(x)+(g+h)(x)\amp \amp\\ \amp =(f+(g+h))(x)\amp \amp . \end{align*}
