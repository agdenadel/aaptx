Since \(e\in H\text{,}\), \(H\neq \emptyset\text{.}\). Next, let \(\sigma, \tau\in H\text{.}\). Since \(\tau(m)=m\) and \(\tau\) is a bijection, \(\tau^{-1}(m)=m\text{.}\). So \(\sigma \tau^{-1}(m)=\sigma(\tau^{-1}(m))=\sigma(m)=m\text{.}\). So \(\sigma \tau^{-1}\in H\text{;}\); therefore, \(H\leq S_n\text{,}\), by the 4.2.5.
