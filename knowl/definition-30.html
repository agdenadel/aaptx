Definition3.2.1

Let \(\langle S,*\rangle\) and \(\langle S',*'\rangle\) be binary structures. A function \(\phi\) from \(S\) to \(S'\) is a homomorphism if \begin{equation*} \phi(a* b)=\phi(a)*'\phi(b) \end{equation*} for all \(a,b\in S\text{.}\) An isomorphism is a homomorphism that is also a bijection.
