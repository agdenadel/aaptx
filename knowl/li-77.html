  • The first thing you should do is check to make sure that \(\langle G,*\rangle\) is a binary structure by making sure \(G\) is closed under \(*\)—if not, it doesn't make sense to check to see if axioms \(\G_1\)–\(\G_3\) hold). (For instance: \(\Z^*\) has no chance of being a group under \(\div\text{,}\) since, e.g., \(3,4\in \Z^*\) but \(3 \div 4 \not\in\Z^*\text{.}\))
