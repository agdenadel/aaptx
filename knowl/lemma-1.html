Lemma2.1.8

(Clock Lemma) Let \(G\) be a group and let \(a \in G\text{.}\) Suppose there is a positive power of \(a\) which equals the identity element \(e\) of \(G\text{.}\) Let \(n\) be the least such positive integer. Then for all \(s,t\in \Z\text{,}\) \(a^s=a^t\) if and only if \(s\) is congruent to \(t\) modulo \(n\text{.}\)
