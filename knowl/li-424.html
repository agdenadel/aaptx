Item7.4.4.b

  1. The left and the right cosets of \(H=\langle 4\rangle =\{0,4,8,12,16\}\) in \(\Z_{20}\) are \(H\text{,}\) \(1+H=\{1,5,9,13,17\}=H+1\text{,}\) \(2+H=\{2,7,10,14,18\}=H+2\text{,}\) and \(3+H=\{3,8,11,15,19\}=H+3\text{.}\)

  2. \(H \unlhd \Z_{20}\text{.}\)

  3. \((\Z_{20}:H)=4\text{.}\)
