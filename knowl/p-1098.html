Since \(|G|\) is prime, \(|G|>1\text{,}\), so there exists \(a\in G\) with \(a\neq e\text{.}\). Then \(\langle a\rangle\) is a subgroup of \(G\text{,}\), so by 7.3.6, \(|\langle a\rangle |\) divides \(|G|\text{.}\). But \(|G|\) is prime and \(a\neq e\text{,}\), so we must have \(|\langle a\rangle |=|G|\text{;}\); thus, \(G=\langle a\rangle\text{.}\).
