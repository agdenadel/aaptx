We first show that \(D_3\) is closed under composition. As noted above, \(rf=fr^2\text{.}\) So any map of the form \(f^ir^jf^kr^l\) (\(i,k=0,1\text{,}\) \(j,l=0,1,2\)) can be written in the form \(f^sr^t\) for some \(s,t \in \N\text{.}\) Finally, let \(R_2(s)\) and \(R_3(t)\) be the remainders when you divide \(s\) by \(2\) and \(t\) by \(3\text{;}\) then \(f^sr^t=f^{R_2(s)}r^{R_3(t)} \in D_3\text{.}\) So \(D_3\) is closed under composition.
