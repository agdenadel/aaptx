In \(\M_2(\R)\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \left\langle \left[ \begin{array}{cc} 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 1 \end{array} \right]\right\rangle =\left\{\left[ \begin{array}{cc} c \amp c \\ 0 \amp c \end{array} \right] : c\in \Z \right\}= \left\{c\left[ \begin{array}{cc} 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 1 \end{array} \right] : c\in \Z \right\}. \end{equation*}

The order of the the matrix is therefore infinity.
