  1. Define a function \(\phi\) from \(G\) to \(G'\) (or from \(G'\) to \(G\text{,}\) as we have TheoremÂ 3).

  2. Prove that \(\phi\) is a homomorphism.

  3. Prove that \(\phi\) is a bijection.
