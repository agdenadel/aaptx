Next, function composition is always associative, and the function \(e\) clearly acts as identity element in \(D_3\text{.}\) Finally, let \(x=f^ir^j\in D_3\text{.}\) Then \(y=r^{3-j}f^{2-i}\) is in \(D_3\) (since \(D_3\) is closed under composition) with \(xy=yx=e\text{.}\) So \(D_3\) is a group.
