Solution2.2.5.1

Let \(u,v\in H\text{.}\) To show \(u*v\in H\text{,}\) we need to show that \((u*v)*(u*v)=u*v\text{.}\) Now, \begin{align*} (u*v)*(u*v)\amp =u*(v*u)*v \amp \amp \text{ (since \(*\) is associative) }\\ \amp =u*(u*v)*v \amp \amp \text{ (since \(*\) is commutative) }\\ \amp =(u*u)*(v*v) \amp \amp \text{ (since \(*\) is associative) }\\ \amp =u*v \amp \amp \text{ (since \(u,v\in H\)) } . \end{align*}

So \(u*v\in H\text{.}\)
