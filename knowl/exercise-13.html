Exercise2

Let \(G\) be the set of all functions from \(\Z\) to \(\R\text{.}\) Prove that pointwise multiplication on \(G\) is commutative. (Note. To prove that two functions, \(h\) and \(j\text{,}\) sharing the same domain \(D\) are equal, you need to show that \(h(x)=j(x)\) for every \(x\in D\text{.}\))
