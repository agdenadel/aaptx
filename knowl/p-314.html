For instance, considering multiplication on \(\R^*\text{,}\), matrix multiplication on \(GL(2,\R)\text{,}\), and addition modulo \(6\) on \(\Z_6\text{,}\), we have that \(\langle \R^*\times GL(2,\R) \times \Z_6,*\rangle\) is a group in which, for instance, \begin{equation*} \left(-1, \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp \phantom{-}3 \\ 0 \amp -1 \end{bmatrix}, 3\right) *\left(\pi, \begin{bmatrix} 2 \amp 1 \\ 1 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix}, 4\right)=\left(-\pi, \begin{bmatrix} \phantom{-}5 \amp \phantom{-}4 \\ -1 \amp -1 \end{bmatrix} ,1\right). \end{equation*}
