Theorem2.5.11

If \(\Gdot\) is a group, then left and right cancellation laws hold in \(G\text{.}\) That is, if \(a,b,c\in G\text{,}\) then

  1. If \(ab=ac\text{,}\) we have \(b=c\) (the left cancellation law); and

  2. If \(ba=ca\text{,}\) we have \(b=c\) (the right cancellation law).
