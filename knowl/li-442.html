Item7.4.4.e.i

  • The left cosets of \(H\) in \(D_4\) are \(H=\{e,f\}\text{,}\) \(rH=\{r,fr^3\}\text{,}\) \(r^2H=\{r^2,fr^2\}\text{,}\) and \(r^3H=\{r^3, fr\}\text{.}\) The right cosets of \(H\) in \(D_4\) are \(H\text{,}\) \(Hr=\{r, fr\}\text{,}\) \(Hr^2=\{r^2,fr^2\}\text{,}\) and \(Hr^3=\{r^3, fr^3\}\text{.}\)
