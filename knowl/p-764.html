Given a \(k\)-cycle \(\sigma=(a_1 a_2\cdots a_k)\text{,}\), there are \(k\) different expressions for \(\sigma\text{.}\). Indeed, we have \begin{equation*} \sigma=(a_1 a_2\cdots a_k)=(a_2 a_3 \cdots a_k a_1)=(a_3 a_4 \cdots a_k a_1 a_2)=\cdots = (a_k a_1 \cdots a_{k-1}). \end{equation*}
