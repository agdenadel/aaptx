We want to show that \(\phi(a)^{-1}=\phi(a^{-1})\text{.}\). Notice that \begin{align*} \phi(a^{-1})\phi(a)\amp =\phi(a^{-1}a)\amp \amp \text{ (since \(\phi\) is a homomorphism) }\\ \amp =\phi(e)\amp \amp \text{(by definition of \(a^{-1}\))}\\ \amp =e'\amp \amp \text{ (since \(\phi(e)\) is the identity element of \(G'\)) }\\ \amp =\phi(a)^{-1}\phi(a)\amp \amp \text{(by definition of \(\phi(a)^{-1}\))} . \end{align*}
