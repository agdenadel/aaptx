Let \(G\) be a group.

  1. If \(H\) is a subgroup of \(G\) then the identity element \(e_H\) of \(H\) is \(e_G\text{,}\) the identity element of \(G\text{.}\)

  2. If \(H\) is a subgroup of \(G\) and \(a\in H\) has inverse \(a^{-1}\) in \(G\text{,}\) then \(a\)'s inverse in \(H\) is also \(a^{-1}\text{.}\)
