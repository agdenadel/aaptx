  • The set \(\{1,2,3\}\) has 5 partitions: namely, \begin{equation*} \{\{1,2,3\}\},\{\{1,2\},\{3\}\}, \{\{1,3\},\{2\}\},\{\{2,3\},\{1\}\} \text{ and } \{\{1\},\{2\},\{3\}\}. \end{equation*} The first partition we've mentioned has one cell, the next three have two cells, and the last one has three cells.
