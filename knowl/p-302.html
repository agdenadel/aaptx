Let \(F\) be the set of all functions from \(\R\) to \(\R\text{,}\) and define pointwise addition \(+\) on \(F\) by

\begin{equation*} (f+g)(x)=f(x)+g(x) \end{equation*}

for all \(f,g\in F\) and \(x\in \R\text{.}\) We claim that \(F\) is a group under pointwise addition. Indeed, if \(f,g\in F\) then clearly \(f+g\) is also a function from \(\R\) to \(\R\text{,}\) so \(F\) is closed under \(+\text{.}\)
