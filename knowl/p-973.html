Let \(G\) be a group with \(H\leq G\) and \(a,b\in G\text{.}\).

  1. The left [right] cosets of \(H\) in \(G\) partition \(G\text{.}\)

  2. \begin{equation*} b\in aH \Leftrightarrow aH=bH \Leftrightarrow a\in bH \end{equation*} and \begin{equation*} b\in Ha \Leftrightarrow Ha=Hb \Leftrightarrow a\in Hb. \end{equation*} In particular, \begin{equation*}a\in H \Leftrightarrow aH=H \Leftrightarrow Ha=H.\end{equation*}

  3. \(H\) is the only left or right coset of \(H\) that is a subgroup of \(G\text{.}\)

  4. \(|aH|=|H|=|Ha|\text{.}\)
