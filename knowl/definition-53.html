Definition6.5.5

Let \(n\) be an integer greater than or equal to \(3\text{.}\) We let \(D_n\) be the collection of symmetries of the regular \(n\)-gon. It turns out that \(D_n\) is a group (see below), called the dihedral group of order \(2n\).
